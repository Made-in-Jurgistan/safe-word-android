How It Works

Just speak naturally. Safe Word listens for commands anywhere in your speech — even at the end of a sentence — and handles them automatically.

What you say What happens
A command on its own
"Delete last word"		 Recognised instantly and executed — nothing is typed.
A command at the end of a sentence
"I'll finish this later. Delete last sentence." 		The dictation is inserted first, then the command runs automatically.
A creative rephrasing
"Trash the whole sentence"		 Safe Word works out the verb (trash = delete) and the target (sentence) and does the right thing.
A command with filler words
"Um, so, delete last word"		 Filler words like um, uh, so, like, well at the start are ignored automatically.
💡
You can always add a wake word or be polite Prefixes like "Safe Word, …", "OK Safe Word …", "please …", "can you …", and trailing sign-offs like "… thanks" are all stripped automatically. So "Safe Word, please delete last word, thanks" works just fine.

9 Find & Replace

Replaces the first match in whatever you're typing into — not case-sensitive. Say the old and new text clearly and Safe Word extracts them from your speech.

Spoken pattern Action

"find X and replace with Y" / "find X and replace it with Y" Longer form — useful when X contains the word "with"

Examples

You say Effect
